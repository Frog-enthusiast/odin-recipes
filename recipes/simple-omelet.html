Simple omelet

This simple omelet is nothing to look down on! It's very simple to make and goes great with toast and jam or a bowl of fruit. Here's what you need to get started:

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Shredded cheese of choice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Some olive oil
  • Optional: about 1TBS diced green pepper and red onion
  • Optional: hot sauce of your choice (I like sriracha)

Directions:

  1. Add olive oil to medium pan and put on medium heat
  2. Crack the 2 eggs and beat in a shallow bowl. Pour into pan and cover for 3 minutes
  3. Add light amount of shredded cheese and add diced veggies. Salt and pepper to taste
  4. Flip over half of omelet and serve
